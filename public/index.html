About

Music festivals are a ton of fun. But being responsible is important as well.

OutsideHelp is a free service that connects you with licensed medical professionals at OutsideLands. It's completely free and anonymous, and is much more convenient and interactive than a phone call. We'll give you an ETA, and tell you how far off help is. And you won't have to yell over your favorite band either. Instead of looking for help, help will be looking for you.

Whenever you're not feeling well, get some free and friendly OutsideHelp.

OutsideHelp was built at OutsideHacks 2015. It makes use of the Weather Underground API, Angular.js, Node.js, CouchDB, and Google Material Design Lite
